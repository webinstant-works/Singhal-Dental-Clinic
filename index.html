FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is dental treatment painful?
+

Modern dental procedures are largely pain-free. We use the latest local anaesthesia techniques and sedation options to ensure your comfort throughout. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable their treatment feels at Singhal Dental Care.

Do you accept appointments, or do I need to walk in?
+

We accept both appointments and walk-ins. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment to avoid waiting time. You can book online, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp — whichever is most convenient for you.

What dental services are available at your clinic?
+

We offer 15+ dental services including Root Canal Treatment, Dental Implants, Orthodontics (braces), Scaling, Extractions, Crown & Bridges, Dentures, Pedodontics, Oral Surgery, and much more. We also provide general medical and homeopathic consultations.

Do you handle dental emergencies?
+

Yes, we prioritize dental emergencies. Whether it's severe toothache, a broken tooth, or a dental injury — call us immediately at +91 92132 43582. We make every effort to see emergency patients the same day.

How often should I visit the dentist?
+

We recommend visiting the dentist every 6 months for a routine checkup and professional cleaning. Regular visits help catch problems early, saving you time, discomfort, and expense in the long run.
